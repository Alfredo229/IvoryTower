A system to the cycles of 6 and 7 has a relative offset of 1. 6 to 7 is 1. 12 to 14 is to. The increment is 1.

A system to the cycles of 5 and 7 has a relative offset of 2. 5 and 7 is 2. 10 to 14 is 4. The increment is 2.

In order ot observe this, draw the increments. 6 12 18... And 7 14 21.

Then, write down the increments. 1 to 6 is 5. 6 to 7 is 1. 7 to 12 is 5.

The sequence is symetric.

Cycle syncronization is at the multiplication of the cycles length. 6 to 7 is 42. 6 sets of 7, or 7 sets of 6.
